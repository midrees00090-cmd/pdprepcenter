FAQ

Common Questions

Quick answers to frequently asked questions

How quickly can you start handling my fulfillment?

Most clients are onboarded within 24-48 hours after the initial consultation. For standard services with no special requirements, we can often start receiving your inventory within 48 hours.

Do you offer volume discounts?

Yes, we offer tiered pricing with volume discounts. As your order volume increases, your per-order costs decrease. Contact us for specific pricing based on your projected volumes.

What e-commerce platforms do you integrate with?

We integrate with all major platforms including Shopify, WooCommerce, Amazon Seller Central, eBay, Walmart, BigCommerce, and custom solutions via API. We can also handle manual order imports if needed.

Can I visit your facility before signing up?

Absolutely! We encourage prospective clients to tour our Philadelphia facility. You can see our operations firsthand and meet our team. Contact us to schedule a tour.

What happens if there's an error with my order?

We maintain a 99.8% accuracy rate. In the rare event of an error, we immediately correct it at our expense, including reshipping the correct item and covering any associated costs.
