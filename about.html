Our Story

Born in Philadelphia, built for e-commerce. We're not just a fulfillment center - we're your logistics partner in growth.

OUR JOURNEY

From Local Startup to Philadelphia's Premier Prep Center

Founded in 2018 by e-commerce entrepreneurs who faced the same logistics challenges you do, Philadelphia Prep Center was born out of necessity. We saw local businesses struggling with fulfillment bottlenecks, high costs, and complex Amazon requirements.

What started as a small warehouse in North Philadelphia has grown into a state-of-the-art fulfillment center serving brands across the country. But our heart remains in Philadelphia - we're committed to supporting local businesses and contributing to our city's thriving e-commerce ecosystem.
